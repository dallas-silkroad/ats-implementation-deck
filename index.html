Phase 02 · Step 04
04
Intelligent Document Processing & Drake Integration
Week 2 – 3
Drake Tax Workaround 1
Why a workaround is required

Drake Tax is a closed system — direct AI integrations into the tax engine aren't supported. Our IDP pipeline solves this by transforming raw client documents into Drake-ready outputs: native import files where available, structured CSVs for bulk entry, and AI-generated data-entry checklists that mirror Drake's screen-by-screen flow.

SR Silk Road Action
Configure OCR and AI extraction agents for common tax documents (W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, brokerage statements). Develop Drake-ready output formats.
ATS ATS Action
Provide anonymized sample documents for AI training. Test output formats with Drake Tax and confirm fit with existing data-entry routines.
OutputIDP system fully operational, feeding Drake-ready data into your established workflow.

05
AI Tax Strategy Consultant Activation
Week 3 – 4
Drake Tax Workaround 2
A parallel advisory layer — not a Drake plugin

Because Drake doesn't expose a strategy-layer API, the AI Tax Strategy Consultant lives alongside Drake as a dedicated advisory interface. Your team queries client scenarios in natural language and receives strategic guidance, scenario modeling, and citation-backed reasoning that can then be implemented inside Drake manually.

SR Silk Road Action
Activate and configure the specialized AI Tax Strategy Consultant interface for ATS, tuned to the practice's typical client profile.
ATS ATS Action
